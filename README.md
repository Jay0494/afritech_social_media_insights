AfriTech is a fast-growing electronics brand operating across multiple digital platforms. Despite strong market presence and high customer engagement, the company experienced **declining revenue, increased product recalls, and rising customer complaints** in 2023.

2. **Rising Customer Complaints**
   Product defects, billing issues, and slow support response times resulted in customer frustration and declining satisfaction.

3. **Product Recalls**
   Media coverage of product recalls amplified negative sentiment and heightened stakeholder concern.

4. **Competitive Pressure**

These challenges highlighted the urgent need for a **centralized, data-driven social media monitoring and crisis-response analytics framework**.

### 6.1 Reputation Risk Is Being Sustained by Slow Response Times

### 6.2 Product Recalls Are Directly Suppressing Revenue

* Overall product recall rate: **49.3%**

**Insight:**
Revenue decline is driven more by **trust erosion** than demand weakness.

## 8. Recommendations

1. **Implement Real-Time Social Listening**

   * Track sentiment, complaint spikes, and recall mentions in near real time.
2. **Set Crisis SLA Benchmarks**

   * Reduce response time from months to weeks or days.
3. **Prioritize High-Risk Products**

   * De-emphasize recall-prone products in campaigns until issues are resolved.
4. **Link Sentiment to Revenue KPIs**

   * Monitor how sentiment shifts impact AOV, repeat purchases, and churn.
5. **Early-Warning Crisis Dashboard**

   * Create alert thresholds for sentiment drops, complaint spikes, and competitor mention surges.
